Air Freight

Speed Without Compromise

When time is the deciding factor, our air freight solutions deliver your cargo to any corner of the globe with unmatched reliability and precision.

Global Air Freight, Locally Managed

Fresh Global Solutions partners with leading international airlines and cargo carriers to offer competitive air freight rates from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and Wilson Airport to over 200 destinations worldwide. Whether you need express next-day delivery or cost-effective consolidation services, our team structures the right solution for your shipment.

Our air freight specialists handle every detail — from booking and documentation to customs clearance and final-mile delivery — so your cargo moves seamlessly from origin to destination without interruption.

  • Express and standard air freight options
  • Air cargo consolidation (LCL/groupage)
  • Charter flights for oversized or urgent cargo
  • Full airway bill management and documentation
  • Pre-clearance and customs facilitation at JKIA
  • Real-time flight and cargo tracking
  • Door-to-door delivery across East Africa

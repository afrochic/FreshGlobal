Perishables

Freshness Delivered. Every Time.

Unbroken cold chain logistics that preserve the quality, freshness, and value of your perishable cargo from farm to final destination.

Kenya's Leading Cold Chain Freight Specialists

Kenya is one of the world's largest exporters of fresh cut flowers, vegetables, and tropical fruits, with millions of tonnes of perishable cargo moving through Nairobi's air freight corridors to Europe, the Middle East, and Asia every year. Fresh Global Solutions has built a dedicated perishables division that understands the precise time and temperature requirements of this highly sensitive cargo category.

Our cold chain solutions cover every link from farm gate to aircraft hold. We coordinate pre-cooling facilities, temperature-controlled road transport, cool room storage at JKIA, priority airline bookings, and phytosanitary documentation — all managed to the exacting standards required by international buyers, import authorities, and major retailers.

  • End-to-end cold chain management
  • Priority air freight bookings on perishables lanes
  • Temperature-controlled vehicles for farm collection
  • Phytosanitary and KEPHIS inspection facilitation
  • Cool room facilities at JKIA cargo terminal
  • Flowers, fresh produce, and frozen goods expertise
  • Real-time temperature monitoring throughout transit
Why Our Cold Chain

Benefits of Our Perishables Service

Protecting the quality and value of your fresh cargo at every stage of its journey to market.

Unbroken Cold Chain

Our infrastructure and protocols ensure no temperature excursions occur from farm to aircraft, preserving product quality and extending shelf life at destination.

Speed to Market

Our priority airline allotments and pre-planned handling routines minimise transit time, getting your fresh produce to European and Middle Eastern markets as quickly as possible.

Regulatory Expertise

We manage KEPHIS certificates, EU phytosanitary requirements, GlobalGAP compliance documentation, and import permits for all major destination markets.

Temperature Monitoring

Continuous temperature data logging during transit provides traceability and audit trails that satisfy the most demanding quality assurance requirements.

Minimal Cargo Losses

Our experienced handlers, proper packaging guidance, and rapid processing reduce spoilage rates, protecting your revenue and buyer relationships.

Flexible Volume Handling

Whether you export one palette or a full plane, we scale our operations to match your seasonal production volumes, from small farm exporters to large agri-businesses.

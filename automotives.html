Automotives

Precision Logistics for the Automotive Industry

From single vehicles to bulk fleets and spare parts, we deliver the expertise and infrastructure East Africa's automotive sector demands.

End-to-End Automotive Freight Solutions

Kenya is one of East Africa's largest automotive markets, importing tens of thousands of vehicles and hundreds of millions of dollars in spare parts annually. Fresh Global Solutions has developed a dedicated automotive logistics unit to serve this high-value, high-volume sector with the precision it requires.

We handle vehicle imports from Japan, the UAE, the UK, and other major source markets via both Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo) and container shipping. Our team manages the complete import process — including pre-shipment inspection (IVR) coordination, NTSA compliance requirements, customs clearance, and final delivery or yard storage. For spare parts, we offer integrated air and sea freight combined with bonded warehousing and just-in-time distribution.

  • RoRo and container vehicle shipping
  • Pre-shipment inspection (IVR) coordination
  • NTSA compliance and registration support
  • Spare parts air and sea freight
  • Bonded storage for high-value automotive parts
  • Fleet import management for corporate buyers
  • Heavy machinery and construction equipment logistics

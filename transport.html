Your Cargo, Moving Every Mile

From port to warehouse, city to upcountry — our transport network keeps East Africa's supply chains rolling without interruption.

Comprehensive Road Transport Across East Africa

Fresh Global Solutions operates a robust transport and distribution network covering Kenya and the wider East African Community. Our fleet of trucks — ranging from light commercial vehicles to 40-tonne articulated lorries — is maintained to the highest road-safety standards, fully GPS-tracked, and available for both scheduled and on-demand movements.

Whether your cargo needs to travel from the Port of Mombasa to Nairobi's industrial area, from JKIA to an upcountry distribution centre, or across the border into Uganda, Tanzania, or Rwanda, our experienced drivers and operations coordinators ensure safe, on-time delivery every time.

  • Full truck load (FTL) and groupage (LTL) services
  • GPS-tracked fleet with real-time visibility
  • Port-to-warehouse container transport
  • Cross-border haulage to Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and DRC
  • Refrigerated vehicles for temperature-sensitive loads
  • Express courier and last-mile delivery solutions
  • Proof of delivery (POD) documentation for every consignment

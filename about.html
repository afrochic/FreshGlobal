Who We Are

East Africa's Premier Freight Forwarding Partner

Founded in 2016 and headquartered in Nairobi along the Mombasa Road corridor, Fresh Global Solutions Ltd has grown from a focused freight forwarding startup into a comprehensive logistics powerhouse serving businesses across East Africa and beyond.

We specialise in air freight, sea freight, customs brokerage, warehousing, and specialised cargo handling — all delivered with the transparency, speed, and care that modern trade demands. Our deep regional knowledge combined with a global network of partners means your cargo is in capable hands at every point in its journey.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

From a bold idea to a regional logistics leader — here is how we grew.

2016

Founded in Nairobi

Fresh Global Solutions Ltd was established with a vision to simplify international freight forwarding for East African businesses.

2017

First Major Air Freight Contract

Secured our first large-scale air freight partnership, enabling express delivery to European and Asian markets.

2019

Sea Freight & Customs Division Launch

Expanded services to include full container load (FCL) and less than container load (LCL) sea freight, plus in-house customs brokerage.

2021

Specialised Cargo Divisions

Launched dedicated units for perishables, pharmaceuticals, and project logistics to serve high-growth sectors in East Africa.

2023

Regional Warehousing Network

Opened bonded warehousing facilities along the Mombasa Road corridor, providing clients with integrated storage and distribution solutions.

2025

Digital Tracking Platform

Launched a real-time shipment tracking platform, giving clients full visibility into their cargo across all modes of transport.

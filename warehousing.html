Warehousing

Smart Storage, Seamless Distribution

Strategically located warehousing facilities that keep your inventory safe, organised, and ready to move when you need it.

Flexible Warehousing Along Nairobi's Logistics Corridor

Our warehousing facilities are strategically positioned along the Mombasa Road corridor — Nairobi's primary logistics artery — giving clients rapid access to JKIA, the Inland Container Depot, and key distribution routes across East Africa. We offer bonded and general warehousing solutions with flexible lease terms, ensuring you only pay for the space and services you actually use.

Each facility is equipped with modern racking systems, 24-hour CCTV surveillance, secure access control, and a dedicated warehouse management system (WMS) that provides real-time inventory visibility. From simple bulk storage to complex pick-and-pack operations, our team is equipped to handle diverse warehousing requirements.

  • Bonded and general warehousing options
  • Real-time inventory management system (WMS)
  • Pick, pack, and distribution services
  • Cold chain and temperature-controlled storage
  • 24/7 CCTV security and access control
  • Cross-docking and transshipment handling
  • Flexible short-term and long-term lease options
Why Our Warehousing

Benefits of Our Storage Solutions

More than just space — our warehousing services add efficiency, visibility, and flexibility to your supply chain.

Prime Location

Situated on Mombasa Road, our facilities offer direct access to JKIA, the ICD, and all major highways serving Nairobi and the wider region.

Real-Time Visibility

Our WMS gives you 24/7 online access to live inventory counts, movement history, and dispatch records for complete stock transparency.

Bonded Status

Our bonded warehouses allow you to defer customs duties until goods are released, improving cash flow for importers and re-exporters.

Scalable Capacity

Scale your storage footprint up or down in response to seasonal demand peaks, without the overhead of long-term fixed leases.

Value-Added Services

We offer labelling, repackaging, kitting, and quality inspection services within the warehouse, reducing the need for additional handling steps.

High-Security Environment

Round-the-clock security personnel, motion detection systems, and insured storage protect your goods from loss, theft, and damage.

Strategic Logistics Consulting from Practitioners Who Know East Africa

A well-designed supply chain is one of the most powerful competitive advantages a business can have. Fresh Global Solutions brings nearly a decade of hands-on logistics expertise to help organisations across manufacturing, retail, agriculture, healthcare, and the public sector redesign their supply chains for greater efficiency, resilience, and cost-effectiveness.

Unlike generalist consultants, our advisory team works with real operational data from the East African context — understanding port congestion patterns, cross-border challenges, infrastructure gaps, and local regulatory nuances that affect the performance of every supply chain in the region. We translate insight into practical, implementable solutions.

  • End-to-end supply chain mapping and diagnostics
  • Freight cost benchmarking and optimisation
  • Inventory management and demand planning support
  • Mode and route selection analysis
  • Supplier and vendor logistics assessment
  • Supply chain risk and resilience planning
  • Technology and digitisation roadmapping

Project Logistics

Big Moves. Precise Execution.

From oversized machinery to multi-phase infrastructure projects, we plan and execute complex cargo movements that standard logistics cannot accommodate.

Specialist Project Cargo and Heavy Lift Logistics

East Africa is experiencing an infrastructure boom — with major projects in energy, telecommunications, manufacturing, and construction driving demand for sophisticated project logistics capabilities. Fresh Global Solutions has developed a dedicated project cargo division equipped to handle the planning, procurement, and execution of the most demanding transport and logistics challenges in the region.

Our project logistics team specialises in out-of-gauge (OOG) cargo, heavy lift equipment, industrial machinery, power generation units, and modular structures. We combine multi-modal transport planning — integrating sea freight, inland water transport, road haulage, and air cargo as needed — with rigorous project management to ensure your critical equipment arrives on time, within budget, and ready for installation.

  • Out-of-gauge (OOG) and heavy lift cargo solutions
  • Multi-modal transport planning and coordination
  • Route surveys and feasibility assessments
  • Industrial plant and machinery relocation
  • Energy sector logistics (wind, solar, oil & gas)
  • Special permits and escort coordination
  • Just-in-time site delivery for construction projects

Benefits of Our Project Logistics Service

Licensed Customs Agents You Can Trust

Fresh Global Solutions is a fully licensed customs clearing agent in the areas of our operation. Our team of accredited customs professionals has deep expertise in Customs Management, and regional conformity standards, ensuring your declarations are accurate and processed without unnecessary hold-ups.

Benefits of Our Customs Brokerage Service

Compliance, speed, and cost savings — the three pillars of our customs brokerage offering.

Full Regulatory Compliance

Our accredited team ensures every declaration meets KRA, KEBS, and EAC requirements, protecting you from penalties and delays at the border.

Faster Clearance Times

Our established relationships with KRA officers and deep system expertise enable us to consistently achieve same-day or next-day clearances.

Duty Minimisation

We proactively identify applicable exemptions, remissions, and preferential tariff rates under EAC and bilateral trade agreements to lower your duty liability.

Accurate Documentation

Every entry is carefully reviewed for tariff codes, valuations, and origin declarations, eliminating costly errors that can trigger audits or examinations.

Dedicated Account Management

You get a named customs officer assigned to your account who knows your cargo profile and proactively keeps you informed throughout clearance.

EAC Transit Expertise

We handle transit cargo procedures for Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and the DRC, navigating cross-border requirements with precision and efficiency.

Pharmaceuticals & Healthcare

Life-Critical Logistics, Handled with Precision

GDP-compliant pharmaceutical freight solutions that protect product integrity, ensure regulatory compliance, and support better health outcomes across East Africa.

Pharmaceutical Logistics Built on Compliance and Care

The pharmaceutical and healthcare sector demands logistics of the highest precision. A single temperature excursion, documentation error, or regulatory non-compliance can destroy an entire shipment — with potentially serious consequences for patients and healthcare providers. Fresh Global Solutions has invested deeply in building a pharmaceutical logistics capability that meets GDP (Good Distribution Practice) standards and the strict requirements of the Pharmacy and Poisons Board of Kenya.

Our dedicated pharma team handles vaccines, biologics, controlled substances, medical devices, and diagnostic equipment, using validated temperature-controlled packaging, qualified cool chain vehicles, and a rigorous quality management process. Every shipment is accompanied by complete documentation and an unbroken audit trail from origin to final delivery.

  • GDP-compliant cold chain freight (2–8°C and -20°C)
  • Pharmacy and Poisons Board import permit facilitation
  • Validated packaging and qualified cool chain vehicles
  • Controlled substances and narcotics clearance
  • Medical devices and diagnostic equipment handling
  • Continuous temperature data logging and reporting
  • NGO and government humanitarian supply chain support
Why Our Pharma Logistics

Benefits of Our Pharmaceutical Service

Precision, compliance, and care — the three non-negotiables in life-critical healthcare supply chains.

GDP-Compliant Operations

Our processes, facilities, and training align with WHO Good Distribution Practice guidelines, providing the quality assurance pharmaceutical manufacturers and distributors require.

Complete Documentation Trail

Every shipment generates a comprehensive documentation package including temperature logs, chain of custody records, and import permits for full regulatory traceability.

Validated Temperature Control

We use validated packaging solutions and calibrated data loggers to maintain product integrity across all temperature ranges, from ambient to deep-frozen.

Rapid Import Processing

Our relationships with the Pharmacy and Poisons Board and KRA enable us to accelerate import permit processing and customs clearance for urgent medical cargo.

Humanitarian & NGO Support

We have extensive experience supporting UNICEF, WHO, and international NGOs with vaccine and medical supply logistics across the East African region.

Secure Handling Protocols

Our trained pharmaceutical handlers follow strict security procedures for high-value medicines, controlled substances, and sensitive biological materials.
